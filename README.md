### About
Farmlet is a terminal based roguelike game where you play as John, a city 
boy become farmer who inherits his small plot of land from his late grandfather.

### Features
Plant your crops, harvest them, make a profit, expand your farm, upgrade 
your tools, take care of your animals, pet your dog(s), etc. learn the f+ 
language-- Oh yeah, the game does come with its own 
syntax and language to mimic some of the most well known. 

### How to play
<pre>
git clone https://github.com/isGoodSoup/farmlet.git
git cd farmlet
</pre>
### OR
1. Open it with your favorite IDE
2. Run it
3. That's it, enjoy!

### Tips
- Never neglect your crops, always water them when the hydration 
level is min. 1.
- Explore, make mistakes, don't be afraid of losing a run.
- Run this script to plant and water your first crop:
<pre>
plant 0 0 && water 0 0 && sleep
</pre>
